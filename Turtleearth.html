About Turtle Earth

Welcome to Turtle Earth, a global movement dedicated to embracing the wisdom of slowness in our fast-paced world. Just as the turtle carries its home on its back, we believe in carrying mindfulness and intention with us wherever we go.

Our Philosophy

At Turtle Earth, we believe that by slowing down, we can actually move forward more meaningfully. Our philosophy is rooted in:

  • Mindful living
  • Sustainable practices
  • Deep connections with nature and community
  • Appreciation for life's simple pleasures

Turtle Practices

Incorporate these turtle-inspired practices into your daily life:

  1. Start your day with a slow, mindful morning routine
  2. Practice "turtle walking" - slow, deliberate steps in nature
  3. Embrace the art of deep listening in conversations
  4. Cultivate patience through slow-growth hobbies like gardening

Join Our Shell-ter

Connect with like-minded individuals around the world who are committed to living life at a turtle's pace. Share experiences, learn from others, and find support in your slow living journey.
